Presidential Libraries and Museums

Modernizing the user experience

The National Archives hosts and supports 14 presidential library/museum websites. Some sites are pure html while others have already moved into a content management system. There is a varying level of effort involved when maintaining and/or redesigning the sites.

Nixon Library and Museume Eisenhower Library and Museume

My Role

I worked closely with executive stakeholders for the libraries and at NARA to define project success. I was in charge of strategy development, multi-team communication, migration planning, content modeling, UX research, information architecture, Drupal theme development, editor development based on the Paragraphs module, and accessibility checks. The following are the key roles I played on the project:

  • Product Manager
  • UX Researcher
  • UI Designer
  • Front-end Developer
  • Data Modeling
  • Information Architecture
  • Drupal Development
  • Accessibility and Quality Checks
