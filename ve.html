Veterans' Experience at the National Archives

Improving access to military records

The National Archives keeps records of military service and medical records for the United States Armed Forces. With over one million requests made and fulfilled yearly, Veterans are one of NARA's largest customers. While we did not have the ability to change the actual system for requests, we were able to set clearer expectations for Veterans and their families, reulting in less email communications and better access to records.
